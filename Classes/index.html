Casa Mila
Casa Batlló
This iconic park was designed by the famous Antoni Gaudi and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It is filled with amazing gardens, giant lizard features, and colorful buildings.
Casa Mila, also known as La Pedrera, is located within a few minutes of Casa Batlló. The building has a unique limestone façade and a constant curve. It is famous for its rooftop terrace so if you do go inside be sure to get up there
The colorful building has a variety of shapes along with irregular oval windows. Much of it is decorated with colorful mosaics made of broken ceramic tiles
